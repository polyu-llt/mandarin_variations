This paper describes the system we developed for DMT in VarDial 2019: Discriminating between Mainland and Taiwan variation of Mandarin Chinese.

The task aims at discriminating between two major varieties of Mandarin Chinese: Putonghua (Mainland China) and Guoyu (Taiwan).
Two versions must be analyzed, the traditional and the simplified. 

----------------- System Description

Se realiza un preprocesamiento muy simple para preservar todas las caracter√≠sticas que pueden ser discriminativas entre los dos tipos de textos que se analizan. Por lo tanto, en este paso solo representamos los textos como vectores tf-idf.

The vectors obtained in the preprocessing are used as input of the model we propose. This model consists of a fully connected neural network of 4 dense layers with the relu activation function, and one output layer with the softmax.

We used just the provide data from this competition.
